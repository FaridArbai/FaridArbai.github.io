Wearable Presenter is a presentation controller app for wearable devices that substitutes the traditional pointer-based presenter, therefore allowing you to free your hands and enrich your presentation with your body language.

Meet Wearable Presenter.

Boost your presentations

A multifunctional tool to manage your presentations in any wearable device, from smartwatches to digital bands.

Slides Control

Move forward to the next slide by tapping the screen or return to the previous slide by double tapping the screen

Time Management

Set up the duration to be aware of the remaining time at any moment. Configure time points to be notified when a specific ammount of time is reached with a silent vibration on your wrist

Multiplatform Support

Use your favourite presentation apps, almost every platform is supported by Wearable Presenter

Real Time

Wearable Presenter works over pure 802.X Wi-fi, which allows it to be much more faster and reliable than the traditional pointer presenter

Device Friendly

This app runs on any device that has Android Wear as its OS, regardless of its hardware or shape

Open Source

Built under a GPL 3.0 license, the code of this app is available on github for anyone to contribute or suit to their particular needs

Free App

Wearable Presenter solves a universal necessity, therefore it is provided for the community to enjoy at no cost
