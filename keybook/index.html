About KeyBook

Real-time service

Built in pure C++, with no middleware and at the top of a customized protocol, Keybook provides with one of the fastest messaging services available

Extremely secure

KeyBook allows the users to create their own point-to-point encryption keys without relying on any centralized entity, therefore providing with a much more secure service than the one offered by Whatsapp, Telegram or Signal

Cross Platform

Its C++ nature allows it to be available for almost any platform, currently supporting Windows, Linux and Android for phone, tablet and desktop devices

Lead your own privacy, trust in no entity

Instead of relying on a centralized key-exchange protocol, KeyBook Messaging allows you to set your encryption keys for each one of your contacts. Therefore, our service ensures that only you and the people you communicate with can read your messages, and no additional entities in between, not even us. Also, unlike nowadays leading platforms such as Whatsapp, Telegram or Signal, our servers do not take part in the process of creating your keys, only you and your contacts. As a consequence, we can not trick you in any way in order to get your keys since we don't take part in their generation process.
