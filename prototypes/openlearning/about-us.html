About Us

Home About Us

Achieving globalized access to learning resources

No cost, compromise or constraints. Learn anything, anywhere at anytime.

A learning platform for anyone to learn or teach. No matter your profile, situation or languange, we believe learning should be available for anyone and thus we provide a centralized platform for everyone to share knowledge.
We take care of our content quality by our highly qualified psychopedagogue department and always make sure that we provide updated resources that are useful to nowadays society.
We deeply believe that free resources are a must to provide an open globalized learning service.
We are fully commited to the Sustainable Development Goals of 2020, not only providing free learning resources but targeting part of our budget to provide learning tools to those who can't afford it and therefore allow more people to access information.

OpenLearning is an initiative that started out as a Masters Degree project. Motivated to provide a solution to the Global Learning problem, our thorough convergence-divergence process applied using the Design Thinking Methodology led us to the conclussion that an open globalized learning platform should be built in order to provide free access to the information

As a student, in OpenLearning you'll have access to a huge set of free learning courses on cutting edge topics, taught by experienced professionals in a variety of multimedia resources. Also, if you'd like to learn something practical but still don't know what, we provide you with a blog where our highly talented psychopedagogues post the trending knowledge topics that are highly useful nowadays.

As an instructor, OpenLearning allows you to upload your content at no cost. It will be tagged with ads by our advertising algorithm so that we can generate a sustainable revenue to support this platform as well as your work. You'll be given 80% of the pay-per-view earnings of each ad inserted in your documents, while 10% of it will be targeted to buy learning tools for those students who need it and the other 10% will be used to support the growth of this platform.
